I am a Robotics and Control Systems Analyst at Intuitive Surgical.

Before joining Intuitive Surgical, I earned my Ph.D. at New York University, where I was co-advised by Giuseppe Loianno (now at UC Berkeley) and Farokh Atashzar (now in industry).

- **Ph.D. lab:** [Medical Robotics and Interactive Intelligent Technologies (MERIIT) Lab](https://wp.nyu.edu/meriit/), New York University
- **Ph.D. advisors:** [Giuseppe Loianno](https://aero.berkeley.edu/people/giuseppe-loianno/) and Farokh Atashzar

Earlier, I was fortunate to explore the intersection of robotics and healthcare during my master's studies in the Rehabilitation Robotics Lab at the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC). As an undergraduate, I conducted research at the Guangdong–Hong Kong Robotics Academy under the guidance of Zexing Li.

- **Master's lab:** Rehabilitation Robotics Lab, University of Illinois Chicago
- **Undergraduate research:** Guangdong–Hong Kong Robotics Academy · **Advisor:** Zexing Li
